Every Dog Deserves a Second Act

At Second Chance Pup Foundation, we believe a dog’s past shouldn't dictate their future. We rescue, nurture, and rehome dogs across Gauteng, helping them transition from abandoned to adored.

Welcome to the Foundation

We are a passionate team dedicated to giving dogs the love, medical care, and training they deserve. Every rescue that enters our doors is met with patience and kindness as we prepare them for their forever home.

Our goal is simple: The perfect match for every dog.

Our Mission

We provide safe shelter and holistic support for every dog in our care. From the first vet check-up to the joyous moment they find their forever family, we are there every step of the way.

By working closely with the Centurion community, we promote responsible pet ownership and strive to create a world where every tail wags with joy.
